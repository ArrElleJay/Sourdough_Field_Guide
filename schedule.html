This gives you a practical planning timeline. Dough still gets the final vote. Warm kitchens move faster, cool kitchens move slower.

Common sense clause: this scheduler assumes an established starter, a normal room-temp kitchen, and dough that is behaving reasonably. If your dough is not ready, wait. If it is sprinting, move faster or use the fridge.
