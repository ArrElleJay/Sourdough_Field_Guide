Schedules

Build a bake day that does not require psychic powers.

Pick a recipe, choose the day and bake window, and get a printable checklist that starts where the recipe actually starts. The scheduler avoids middle-of-the-night starter feeds where it can, because bread is great but 3 AM is stupid.

Bake Day Scheduler

Build a timeline from starter to oven

This works backward from your bake day and bake window. Recipe timing stays recipe-based; the starter build may adjust ratio or timing to keep the schedule human.

Common sense clause: this scheduler uses recipe-based timing and then nudges starter feeding into normal human hours when possible. If your dough is not ready, wait. If it is sprinting, move faster or use the fridge.

Fridge bake

Use this when you shape the dough, refrigerate it, and bake later. Bake straight from cold. Cleaner scoring, easier timing, less drama.
