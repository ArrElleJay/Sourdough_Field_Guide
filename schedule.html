This works backward from the time you want to bake. Recipe default uses the timing the recipe calls for; sourness options adjust the plan when that makes sense.

Common sense clause: this scheduler assumes normal room temperature, recipe-default timing, and dough that is behaving reasonably. If your dough is not ready, wait. If it is sprinting, move faster or use the fridge.

Same-day bake

Use this when you want the loaf baked today. It is usually milder and depends on a warm enough kitchen and a starter that is actually ready.

Fridge bake

Use this when you shape the dough, refrigerate it, and bake later. Bake straight from cold. Cleaner scoring, easier timing, less drama.
