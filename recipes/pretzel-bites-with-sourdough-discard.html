Pretzel Bites with Sourdough Discard

Makes 32 bites | Bake 500°F for 8 to 10 minutes | Rise 1 to 1 1/2 hours plus 10 to 15 minutes

Pretzel Bites with Sourdough Discard

Makes 32 bites | Bake 500°F for 8 to 10 minutes | Rise 1 to 1 1/2 hours plus 10 to 15 minutes

Back to recipes

Ingredients

AmountIngredient
145gsourdough discard or bubbly starter
175glukewarm water
5ggranulated sugar
8ginstant yeast
6gsalt
285gall-purpose flour, plus more for rolling
120ghot water for wash
15gbaking soda
55gunsalted butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Mix discard, water, sugar, yeast, salt, and flour.
  2. Knead 5 to 8 minutes by hand, or about 5 minutes in mixer, until smooth. Add flour 1 tbsp at a time only if too sticky.
  3. Lightly oil bowl, turn dough to coat, cover, and rise 1 to 1 1/2 hours until doubled.
  4. Divide dough into 4 sections. Roll each into a long strand. Cut each strand into 6 to 8 bites.
  5. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Dunk each bite in wash.
  6. Preheat oven to 500°F. Let bites rise 10 to 15 minutes while oven heats.
  7. Bake 8 to 10 minutes until crisp and lightly browned.
  8. Brush or toss hot bites with all melted butter. Top with salt, cinnamon sugar, or parmesan.

Recipe card version

  1. Mix and knead dough until smooth.
  2. Rise until doubled. Cut into bites.
  3. Dip in baking soda wash. Rise while oven heats.
  4. Bake 500°F for 8 to 10 minutes. Butter aggressively.
Notes: It will feel like too much butter. It is not too much butter.
