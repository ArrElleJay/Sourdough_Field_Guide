Sourdough English Muffins

Makes 10 | Cook low, covered, 4 minutes per side | Ferment 8 to 12 hours plus 1 hour rise

Sourdough English Muffins

Makes 10 | Cook low, covered, 4 minutes per side | Ferment 8 to 12 hours plus 1 hour rise

Back to recipes

Ingredients

AmountIngredient
100gactive sourdough starter, 1:3.3:3.3
20ghoney, sugar, or maple syrup
240gmilk
360gall-purpose flour
5gfine sea salt
40gcornmeal, for sprinkling only

Instructions

  1. Mix starter, sweetener, milk, flour, and salt until well combined. Do not add cornmeal to dough.
  2. Cover and rest 30 to 60 minutes.
  3. Knead 5 minutes by hand, or use stand mixer with dough hook on lowest speed.
  4. Cover and ferment at room temperature 8 to 12 hours.
  5. Turn onto floured surface and press to 1/2-inch thickness.
  6. Sprinkle parchment-lined sheet with cornmeal. Cut rounds and place on sheet.
  7. Sprinkle tops with cornmeal, cover, and rise 1 hour.
  8. Cook in covered skillet over low heat, 4 minutes per side. Center should register about 200°F.

Recipe card version

  1. Mix dough, rest, knead.
  2. Ferment 8 to 12 hours.
  3. Press to 1/2 inch, cut rounds, cornmeal both sides.
  4. Rise 1 hour. Cook covered on low, 4 minutes per side.
Notes: Warm/humid adjustment: reduce milk by 30g and use sugar instead of honey.
