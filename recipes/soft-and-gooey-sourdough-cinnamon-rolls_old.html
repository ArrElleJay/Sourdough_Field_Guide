Soft and Gooey Sourdough Cinnamon Rolls

Makes 8 rolls | Bake 350°F for 35 to 40 minutes | Overnight dough plus 1 to 2 hour second rise

Soft and Gooey Sourdough Cinnamon Rolls

Makes 8 rolls | Bake 350°F for 35 to 40 minutes | Overnight dough plus 1 to 2 hour second rise

Back to recipes

Ingredients

AmountIngredient
Dough: 160gmilk, whole or 2%
Dough: 28gunsalted butter, melted
Dough: 1large egg
Dough: 100gbubbly active sourdough starter
Dough: 24ggranulated sugar
Dough: 300gflour
Dough: 5gfine sea salt
Filling: 6 tbspunsalted butter, softened
Filling: 100ggranulated sugar
Filling: 1 tbspground cinnamon, or 2 tbsp Ceylon
Filling: 1 level tbspflour
Glaze: 2 tbspunsalted butter, softened
Glaze: 1/3 cupcream cheese, room temp
Glaze: 1/2 cuppowdered sugar, sifted
Glaze: 1 to 2 tbspmilk, as needed

Instructions

  1. Evening: mix melted butter and milk. Let cool slightly.
  2. In stand mixer with paddle, mix egg, starter, and sugar. Slowly pour in milk and butter.
  3. Add flour and salt. Mix about 1 minute until rough and sticky. Cover and rest 30 minutes.
  4. Switch to dough hook. Knead 6 to 8 minutes until soft and pulling from bowl.
  5. Move to buttered bowl, cover, and rise overnight, 8 to 12+ hours at 67 to 68°F, until doubled.
  6. Morning: line 9-inch pan with parchment. Turn dough out, pat into rectangle, and rest 10 minutes.
  7. Roll dough to about 16 x 13 inches.
  8. Mix filling and spread over dough, leaving 1/2-inch border.
  9. Roll from long side into tight log. Cut into 2-inch sections.
  10. Place rolls in lined pan, not touching. Rise 1 to 2 hours until puffy.
  11. Bake center rack at 350°F for 35 to 40 minutes. Cool 15 minutes. Beat glaze and spread over warm rolls.

Recipe card version

  1. Make dough at night. Rest 30 minutes, knead, and bulk rise overnight.
  2. Morning: roll dough, spread filling, roll log, and cut.
  3. Rise 1 to 2 hours. Bake 350°F for 35 to 40 minutes. Glaze warm.
Notes: If the dough resists rolling, rest 5 to 10 minutes and try again. The dough is not the boss of you.
