Sourdough Discard Pancakes

Makes 12 pancakes | Cook medium-low | 2 to 3 minutes first side, 1 to 2 minutes second side

Sourdough Discard Pancakes

Makes 12 pancakes | Cook medium-low | 2 to 3 minutes first side, 1 to 2 minutes second side

Back to recipes

Ingredients

AmountIngredient
240gall-purpose flour
8gbaking powder
5gbaking soda
25ggranulated sugar
6gsalt
227gsourdough discard, room temp
360g2% milk, room temp
1large egg, beaten, room temp
28gvegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Add discard, milk, egg, and oil.
  3. Mix just until combined. A few lumps are fine. Do not overmix.
  4. Grease griddle or cast iron skillet with butter or oil and heat over medium-low.
  5. Add about 1/3 cup batter per pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on top, about 2 to 3 minutes. Flip and cook 1 to 2 minutes more.

Recipe card version

  1. Whisk dry ingredients.
  2. Add discard, milk, egg, and oil. Mix gently.
  3. Cook 1/3 cup batter per pancake on medium-low until bubbles form. Flip and finish.
Notes: Use discard, not bubbly active starter. For waffles, use the batter as written or add 1 extra egg.
