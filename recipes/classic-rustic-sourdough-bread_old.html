Classic Rustic Sourdough Bread

Makes 1 loaf | Bake 500°F covered, then 450°F uncovered | Rise about 6 hours plus 1 to 2 hours

Classic Rustic Sourdough Bread

Makes 1 loaf | Bake 500°F covered, then 450°F uncovered | Rise about 6 hours plus 1 to 2 hours

Back to recipes

Ingredients

AmountIngredient
100gactive sourdough starter
325gwarm water
475gflour, bread flour or high-protein all-purpose
10gsalt

Instructions

  1. Mix starter and water until dissolved. Add flour and salt. Mix until no dry bits remain. Cover and rest 30 minutes.
  2. Do 3 to 4 stretch and folds, 15 minutes apart.
  3. Cover and rise until doubled, about 6 hours at 72°F.
  4. Shape into a tight boule. Place seam-side up in a proofing basket or bowl.
  5. Cover and proof 1 to 2 hours, or refrigerate after shaping for a later bake.
  6. Preheat oven and Dutch oven to 500°F. Score loaf, add a few ice cubes under parchment if using, and cover.
  7. Bake covered 25 minutes. Reduce to 450°F, uncover, and bake until browned and 200 to 210°F inside.
  8. Cool fully before slicing. Yes, fully.

Recipe card version

  1. Mix all dough ingredients. Rest 30 minutes.
  2. Stretch and fold 3 to 4 times, 15 minutes apart.
  3. Bulk ferment until doubled.
  4. Shape, proof, score, bake covered 25 minutes at 500°F, then uncovered at 450°F until 200 to 210°F inside.
Notes: Remove from the Dutch oven immediately after baking to prevent a burned bottom.
