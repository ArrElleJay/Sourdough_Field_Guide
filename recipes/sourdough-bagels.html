Sourdough Bagels

Makes 8 | Bake 425°F for 25 to 28 minutes | Ferment 8 to 12 hours | Boil 2 minutes per side

Sourdough Bagels

Makes 8 | Bake 425°F for 25 to 28 minutes | Ferment 8 to 12 hours | Boil 2 minutes per side

Back to recipes

Ingredients

AmountIngredient
100gactive sourdough starter, 1:3.3:3.3
255gwater
40ghoney or sugar
10gfine sea salt
500gbread flour
6 cupswater for boil
1 tbspgranulated sugar for boil
optionalsesame, everything seasoning, salt, poppy seeds, or shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Stir starter, water, honey or sugar, and salt in mixer bowl.
  2. Add bread flour and bring together by hand. Dough will be stiff.
  3. Knead with dough hook on lowest speed 6 to 7 minutes, or by hand 10 minutes.
  4. Cover and rest at room temperature 8 to 12 hours.
  5. Divide into 8 pieces. Shape each into ball, poke thumb through center, and stretch gently into ring.
  6. Cover and rise 30 to 60 minutes until puffy.
  7. Preheat oven to 425°F. Bring water bath to boil and add sugar.
  8. Boil 2 minutes per side, 3 to 4 at a time. Add toppings while wet.
  9. Bake 25 to 28 minutes until golden brown.

Recipe card version

  1. Mix stiff dough. Knead well.
  2. Ferment 8 to 12 hours.
  3. Shape 8 bagels. Rise until puffy.
  4. Boil 2 minutes per side. Top. Bake 425°F for 25 to 28 minutes.
Notes: Cinnamon raisin option: add 2 tsp cinnamon to dry ingredients and knead in 1/2 cup raisins after kneading.
