Sourdough Cheez-It Crackers

Makes 6 servings | Bake 350°F for 15 to 18 minutes | Chill 20 minutes

Sourdough Cheez-It Crackers

Makes 6 servings | Bake 350°F for 15 to 18 minutes | Chill 20 minutes

Back to recipes

Ingredients

AmountIngredient
226gcheddar cheese, chopped
85gflat sourdough discard
28gunsalted butter, chopped
5granch seasoning
5gsalt, or half for less salty
65gall-purpose flour
optionalflaky sea salt

Instructions

  1. Blend cheddar, discard, butter, ranch seasoning, salt, and flour in food processor until dough comes together.
  2. Wrap and chill at least 20 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  4. Split dough in half and roll very thin, about 1/8 inch, on parchment or silicone mat.
  5. Cut into 1-inch squares. Poke a hole in each cracker.
  6. Sprinkle with flaky salt. Do not separate crackers.
  7. Bake 15 to 18 minutes, rotating trays halfway if needed.
  8. Turn oven off and let crackers sit inside with the door slightly ajar to dry and crisp.

Recipe card version

  1. Blend dough ingredients. Chill 20 minutes.
  2. Roll very thin. Cut squares. Poke centers.
  3. Bake 350°F for 15 to 18 minutes. Dry in turned-off oven.
Notes: Thin dough is the whole game. When it looks thin enough, make it thinner.
