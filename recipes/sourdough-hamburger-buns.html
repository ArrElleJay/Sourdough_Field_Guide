Sourdough Hamburger Buns

Makes 6 buns | Bake 375°F for 25 minutes | Rise about 6 hours plus 1 to 2 hours

Sourdough Hamburger Buns

Makes 6 buns | Bake 375°F for 25 minutes | Rise about 6 hours plus 1 to 2 hours

Back to recipes

Ingredients

AmountIngredient
50gactive sourdough starter, 1:2.5:2.5
360gall-purpose flour
240gwater
30ghoney
10gsalt
1 egg + 1 tbsp wateregg wash
optionalsesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Mix starter, flour, water, honey, and salt until no dry bits remain. Cover and rest 30 minutes.
  2. Do 3 stretch and folds, 30 minutes apart.
  3. Cover and rise until doubled, about 6 hours at 72°F.
  4. Divide into 6 pieces, about 112 to 115g each. Shape into balls and place on parchment-lined sheet.
  5. Cover and rise until puffy, 1 to 2 hours.
  6. Preheat oven to 375°F. Brush tops with egg wash and add sesame seeds if using.
  7. Bake 25 minutes until golden. Cool before slicing.

Recipe card version

  1. Mix dough. Rest 30 minutes.
  2. Do 3 stretch and folds, 30 minutes apart.
  3. Rise until doubled. Divide into 6 buns.
  4. Proof until puffy. Egg wash. Bake 375°F for 25 minutes.
Notes: After the first rise, dough can be refrigerated up to 2 days.
