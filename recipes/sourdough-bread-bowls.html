Sourdough Bread Bowls

Makes 5 to 6 bowls | Bake 450°F for 25 to 30 minutes | Levain overnight plus bulk and cold ferment

Sourdough Bread Bowls

Makes 5 to 6 bowls | Bake 450°F for 25 to 30 minutes | Levain overnight plus bulk and cold ferment

Back to recipes

Ingredients

AmountIngredient
225gripe active levain, 1:10:10
400gwater
600gbread flour
12gsalt
1/2 to 1 cupice cubes

Instructions

  1. Mix ripe levain, water, flour, and salt until shaggy dough forms.
  2. Bulk ferment 4 to 5 hours at 78 to 80°F. Rest 1 hour, then do 3 stretch and fold sets about 1 hour apart.
  3. After final set, cover and rest about 2 more hours. Dough should rise 30 to 40%, show bubbles, pull from bowl edges, and dome toward center.
  4. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Dough can stay cold 8 to 48 hours.
  5. Divide cold dough into 5 to 6 pieces, 200 to 240g each. Shape into tight rounds.
  6. Place rounds on parchment, cover, and rise 2 to 3 hours until puffy and almost doubled.
  7. Preheat baking stone at 450°F for 30 to 45 minutes. Score each dough ball.
  8. Put shallow pan with ice on bottom rack. Bake 15 minutes without opening oven.
  9. Remove steam pan and bake another 10 to 15 minutes until desired color. Cool completely before cutting.

Recipe card version

  1. Mix dough. Bulk ferment with 3 stretch and folds.
  2. Cold ferment 8 to 48 hours.
  3. Divide into 5 to 6 bowls. Rise 2 to 3 hours.
  4. Bake 450°F with steam 15 minutes, then 10 to 15 minutes more.
Notes: No stone: use parchment-lined baking sheet and bake at 450°F for 25 to 30 minutes.
