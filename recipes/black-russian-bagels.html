Black Russian Bagels

Makes about 12 | Bake 425°F for 20 to 22 minutes | Rise 1 hour plus 20 minutes | Boil 1 minute per side

Black Russian Bagels

Makes about 12 | Bake 425°F for 20 to 22 minutes | Rise 1 hour plus 20 minutes | Boil 1 minute per side

Back to recipes

Ingredients

AmountIngredient
420gbread flour
120gdark rye or pumpernickel flour
340mllukewarm water
100gdark treacle or molasses
30gdark cocoa powder, black cocoa ideal
2 tspinstant yeast
2 tspsalt
optional 1 tbspcaraway seeds
optional 1 tspespresso powder
2 qt / 2Lwater for boil
2 tbsptreacle or brown sugar for boil
optionalpoppy seeds, sesame, onion flakes, or everything seasoning

Instructions

  1. Combine dry ingredients in mixer bowl: bread flour, rye flour, yeast, salt, caraway, and espresso powder if using.
  2. Mix lukewarm water, treacle or molasses, and cocoa powder. Pour into dry ingredients and mix into dough. Rest 10 minutes.
  3. Knead by hand 6 to 8 minutes, or with dough hook 4 to 6 minutes, until smooth and elastic. Dough will be dark and slightly sticky.
  4. Cover and rise in warm spot about 1 hour until doubled.
  5. Punch down and divide into 12 pieces, about 80 to 90g each. Shape into balls.
  6. Poke center hole and stretch to about 1 inch. Place on lined, oiled baking sheet.
  7. Cover loosely and rest 20 minutes.
  8. Preheat oven to 425°F. Bring water bath to boil and stir in treacle or brown sugar.
  9. Boil 2 to 3 bagels at a time for 1 minute per side. Add toppings while wet.
  10. Bake 20 to 22 minutes until crust is shiny and dark. Cool on rack.

Recipe card version

  1. Mix dry. Mix water, molasses, and cocoa. Combine and rest 10 minutes.
  2. Knead only until smooth. Rise 1 hour.
  3. Shape 12 bagels. Rest 20 minutes.
  4. Boil 1 minute per side. Bake 425°F for 20 to 22 minutes.
Notes: Hydration and knead were adjusted after a dry/chewy test batch. Retest before treating as final.
